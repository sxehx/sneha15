Introduction

Sacred Heart Pre-University College Madanthyar is a renowned educational institution attracting students from various districts such as Chikamagaluru, Coorg, Dakshina Kannada, and Mysore. Established in 1981 by the late Rev. Fr. Liguory Dsouza, the college received grant-in-aid in 1988 for the PU College and in 1986 for the high school. The college is accredited with an A grade status by the PU Education Department. Currently, there are 579 students enrolled in the PU College and 45 staff members. Rev. Fr. Jerome Dsouza is the current Principal.

Facilities

• The college has excellent infrastructure.
• Experienced teaching faculty and office staff.
• Well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer labs.
• CCTV Surveillance and security guards.
• Hostel facilities for boys and girls.
• Free CET Coaching for minorities, SC, ST, and OBC.
• Literary and co-curricular activities including athletics and games.

Courses

At the PU level, the science combinations are PCMB and PCMC. In Commerce, there are three combinations: HEBA, EBAC, and EBAS. In the Arts section, the combination is HEPS.

Achievements

Overall academic results at PU level in March 2018 were 88%: 3 Toppers, 24 Distinctions, 183 First Class, and 56 Second Class students.
